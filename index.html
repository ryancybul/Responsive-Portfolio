About Me

My name is Ryan and I am currently enrolled in Northwestern University’s Coding Bootcamp. My goal at the end of the program is to become a full-time front-end web developer and I’m really looking forward to it!


I currently work at a great audio visual company in Chicago called Meeting Tomorrow as an inventory control manager. At Meeting Tomorrow I am responsible for managing an inventory of 18,000 assets including thousands of tablets, computers, projectors, sound systems, screens, you name it… Prior to working at Meeting Tomorrow I worked many years in advertising as a post-production sound editor. My advertising career highlight was having the chance to record the voice of Cap’n Crunch. After spending 10+ years in the audio industry I finally decided it’s time to hang up my hat and blaze a new trail with web development.


When I’m not studying at my computer you’ll find me rock climbing, riding my bike, petting cats, or on my back porch brewing beer.


Cheers!
